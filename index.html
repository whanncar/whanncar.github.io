Wade Hann-Caruthers

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Michigan on the Multi-Agent Learning MURI, supervised by Vijay Subramanian. My research is in microeconomic theory, focusing on social learning, social choice, and mechanism design. I study how individuals acquire information in networks, how complex knowledge diffuses, and how mechanisms can improve learning and collective outcomes. I also work on learning in games and modeling strategic interactions involving algorithmic/AI agents.
